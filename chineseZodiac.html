The Lunar Cycle and the Great Race

The Chinese Zodiac (Shengxiao) is a 12-year cycle where each year is represented by an animal. According to legend, the Jade Emperor organized a "Great Race" across a river to determine the order of the zodiac signs. How to Celebrate Your Zodiac Year

In Chinese culture, when it is the year of your zodiac sign (Ben Ming Nian), it is actually considered a year of challenges. To ward off bad luck, people often wear red clothing or red accessories (like a red string bracelet) given to them by an elder.
